Privacy Policy

Coin Scanner  ·  Last updated: 19 June 2026

This policy explains what data Coin Scanner collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

1. Who We Are

Coin Scanner is developed and operated by Daniel Vince. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at danielvince0509@gmail.com.

2. Data We Collect

Account information

  • If you sign up with email and password: your email address and a hashed password (we never store plain-text passwords).
  • If you sign in with Apple: a private relay email address (or your real email if you choose to share it) and a unique Apple user identifier.
  • If you sign in with Google: your Google account email address and a unique Google user identifier.

Coin images

  • Photos you take or upload for coin identification are sent to our server for AI analysis and, if you save the result, stored in your personal collection.
  • Images are stored securely in Supabase Storage and are only accessible to your account.

Collection data

  • Coin identification results (name, country, year, estimated value, condition) that you choose to save.
  • Wishlist items and portfolio entries you create.
  • XP points and level progress associated with your account.

Subscription and purchase data

  • Subscription status and purchase history are managed by RevenueCat. We receive anonymised entitlement data (e.g. "active subscriber") but never your full payment card details.
  • All payment processing is handled by Apple App Store or Google Play Store directly.

Advertising data

  • The app shows optional rewarded ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect your device advertising identifier (IDFA/GAID) and usage signals to serve relevant ads, subject to your device-level ad tracking preferences.
  • You can opt out of personalised ads at any time in your device Settings.

Usage data

  • Scan counts and app activity used to enforce the free scan limit (5 scans per month for free users). This data is stored locally on your device.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and maintain your account.
  • To analyse coin photos using AI and return identification results to you.
  • To store your saved collection, wishlist, and portfolio.
  • To manage your subscription and enforce feature access.
  • To display rewarded ads as an alternative to paid subscriptions.
  • To track XP and level progress within the app.
  • To respond to support enquiries.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Camera and Photo Library

Coin Scanner requests access to your device camera and photo library solely to capture or select coin images for identification. Images are not accessed for any other purpose. Camera access is required for the core scanning feature; photo library access is optional and only used when you tap "Upload Photo".

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — Authentication, database, and image storage. Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — AI vision analysis of coin images. Images are sent to Anthropic's API server-side and are subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management. Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — Rewarded advertisements. Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign In / Google Sign In — Optional social authentication, governed by Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies.

6. Data Retention

Your account data, collection, and coin images are retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data and stored images within 30 days, except where we are required to retain records for legal or financial compliance purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

Coin Scanner is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Data portability (receive your data in a machine-readable format).

To exercise any of these rights, email us at danielvince0509@gmail.com.

9. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), hashed passwords, and row-level security on our database. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security but take reasonable steps to protect your data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
